Codenames - would require two llms simultaneously playing the word-based board game Codenames, measuring accuracy and the highest amount of words attached to one clue.
Bias within Bias - ask LLM to generate statistics of various demographics, test amount of bias when LLM is explicitly prompted to be aware of its biases.
Butterfly effect - prompt LLM to start from a critical event in history but change the result of the event, and test how much the rest of history up to the present changes.
Political bias in interaction with biased "human". One LLM emulate a biased human and explain/summarize a politically charged article and conversate with another LLM, which is measured on its political alignment at every stage of the conversation
